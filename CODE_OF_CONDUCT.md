# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

By participating in this project, you agree to abide by its terms.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the GeoLens community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. We commit to acting in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community — whether you are filing your first issue, reviewing your first PR, or operating GeoLens in production.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project team at **conduct@getgeolens.com**. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
