## Joining

GeoLens is currently maintained by a single person. If you'd like to take on a
maintainer role, the path is described in [GOVERNANCE.md](GOVERNANCE.md#becoming-a-maintainer):
build a track record of merged contributions, and a current maintainer may
nominate you.
