## Decisions Made

- Used `session.refresh(record, attribute_names=["keywords", "contacts", "distributions"])` to eager-load ORM list relationships before calling the synchronous `dataset_to_ogc_record()`. Assigning directly to `record.keywords = []` triggers SQLAlchemy lazy-load in async context → `MissingGreenlet`. The `attribute_names` refresh avoids this.
- Monkeypatched `app.platform.storage.get_storage` (not the script's local reference) so tests 3 and 4 intercept the storage call inside the `reconcile()` coroutine after it imports from the module.
