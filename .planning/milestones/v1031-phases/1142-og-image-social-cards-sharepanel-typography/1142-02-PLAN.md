<objective>
Wire the SHARE-08 frontend half and the SHARE-10 typography pass:
1. Capture a second 1200x630 JPEG inside the existing `doCapture()` render pass (D-04: every save,
   same repaint as the thumbnail) and upload it through the new `PUT /maps/{id}/og-image/` route.
2. Make the canonical copied Share Link the unfurlable `/api/maps/shared/{token}/card` URL (D-02), while
   leaving the `/m/{token}` viewer URL and the embed iframe src unchanged.

   2-explicit-weight hierarchy.

(no `@vercel/og`/`satori` — D-06). The copied link must be the `/card` URL so pasted links unfurl in social
clients; humans are redirected to the viewer by the card's `<meta refresh>`. SHARE-10 closes v1030 audit
finding F2.

Output: `uploadOgImage` API fn, the second capture in `doCapture`, `getShareCardUrl()` wired into Copy Link,
4 font-class edits, and vitest coverage for capture wiring + SharePanel.
