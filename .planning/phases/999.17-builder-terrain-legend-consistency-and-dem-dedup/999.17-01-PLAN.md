<objective>
Fix 1 (Legend consistency, per D-01/D-02/D-03). Make the legend agree with the layer
stack about terrain-suppressed DEM layers, on BOTH the builder `LegendPlugin.tsx` and the
viewer `LayerLegend.tsx` (the viewer derives its entry list via `layer-identity.ts`'s
`createViewerLayerEntries`).

Three behaviors:
1. EXCLUDE per-layer `render_mode:"terrain"` DEM layers from the legend entirely (D-02) ŌĆö
   they have no stack row and paint nothing; today they show as phantom legend entries.
2. ADD a SINGLE synthetic "3D terrain" legend entry driven by `terrain_config` (D-01),
   mirroring the shape of the stack's synthetic `relief:terrain` row (`map-stack.ts`).
3. KEEP hillshade-mode relief layers that actually paint as normal legend entries (D-03).

Per RESEARCH recommendation and Claude's-Discretion, factor ONE shared derivation helper
(`deriveTerrainLegendEntry`) consumed by both surfaces. Both surfaces drive their
terrain-suppression decision off the existing `isDemTerrainVisualSuppressed` predicate
(`map-sync.ts:52`) ŌĆö do NOT re-derive the predicate independently in either legend surface
(D-02). This keeps legend Ōåö stack Ōåö map-sync in lockstep, the phase's core invariant.

Purpose: stop phantom DEM legend entries from recurring on any terrain map; let viewers of
published/shared maps see that 3D terrain is active.
Output: shared helper, both legend surfaces updated, unit tests on both surfaces, new i18n
keys with en/es/fr/de parity.
</objective>
