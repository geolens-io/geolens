GeoLens is not exposed via Ingress. To access it locally, run:

  # Frontend (UI)
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "geolens.fullname" . }}-frontend 8080:8080

  # API (direct)
  kubectl port-forward svc/{{ include "geolens.fullname" . }}-api 8000:8000

Then open http://localhost:8080 in your browser.
