GeoLens software is licensed under Apache-2.0 (see [LICENSE](LICENSE) and
[NOTICE](NOTICE)). This trademark policy is **separate** from the copyright

policy governs use of the GeoLens *name and logo*.

The goal of this policy is to let the community talk about, build on, and
redistribute GeoLens freely, while avoiding confusion about what is and isn't an
official build.

## Permitted without permission

- Nominative and descriptive references — "built with GeoLens", "runs on
  GeoLens", "compatible with GeoLens".
- Unmodified redistribution of official builds under the GeoLens name.
- Truthful statements in articles, talks, tutorials, and documentation.

## Requires a different name

- Distributing a **modified** version under the "GeoLens" name. Forks that
  change the source must use a distinct name; they may truthfully state that
  they are "based on GeoLens".
- Product, company, or domain names that could imply official affiliation or
  endorsement.

## Logo

- Do not alter the logo's proportions, colors, or wording.
- Do not use the logo as your own product or app icon.

## Events and community

- Community meetups and events may reference GeoLens descriptively. Please do
  not imply official sponsorship or endorsement without written permission.

## Questions

If you're unsure whether a use is permitted, open a
[GitHub Discussion](https://github.com/geolens-io/geolens/discussions) or contact
the maintainers at hello@getgeolens.com.
